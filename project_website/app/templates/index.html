About the Project

This project explores the intricate dance between celestial bodies, simulating the gravitational interactions between stars and planets in a dynamic and visually compelling way. Using advanced ray tracing techniques, the simulation captures phenomena like the Doppler effect — illustrating how light shifts as objects move — and the reflection of starlight off planetary surfaces.


By combining accurate physics with real-time simulation, this project offers an interactive way to visualize how light and motion shape the universe. Users can define their own celestial bodies — stars and planets — and watch as the simulation brings their interactions to life. Whether you're interested in astronomy, physics, or the art of light itself, this project provides a fascinating glimpse into the mechanics of space.


This description focuses on the key aspects of your project, including the simulation of gravitational interactions and the visual appeal of ray tracing to capture realistic effects like the Doppler shift and starlight reflection.
